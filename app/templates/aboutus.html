Our Team

Isaiah, Daniel, Aidan, and Ruby are four Oakland Technical High School students in the Computer Science Academy, hoping to make the college application and decision process easier for rising seniors at Tech. As four seniors who have been through the application process, we know the importance of researching schools and understanding as much about the school as possible to help decide which university will be best fit. We aim to reduce the stress and uncertainty many high school students feel by providing them with a forum where they can receive advice from Oakland Tech alumni who have already been through the process and know the best way to go about it, a great way to gain realistic information about each school and individual experiences.

Project Purpose

Connect current Tech students with Tech alumni who have graduated and are now in college. Students will be able to discuss any topic they want with alumni who can provide insight on their current life at university and reflect back on their experiences in high school. Alumni are free to reflect on what they did well or would have changed about their Oakland Tech experience, such as if their course load was challenging enough, if they joined and valued specific clubs, if they used study habits that worked well, and more.
